About RDR2

The most recent major update for Bleach: Rebirth of Souls is version 1.30, released on October 21, 2025, which introduces significant battle system rebalancing and character-specific adjustments. Key changes include refinements to the Reversal Gauge system, modified combo interactions, and new parry mechanics to promote skill-based gameplay. Additionally, several major characters have received updates to improve their responsiveness and flow. Game system changes Reversal Gauge: The system has been refined with adjusted recovery speeds, added partial recovery upon using Spiritual Pressure Moves, and a restriction on when the "Chain Reverse" can be activated to prevent overly strong pressure tactics. Parry mechanics: The speed at which the Reverse Gauge recovers from a parry has been increased, and standardization of cancel frames for parry attacks makes chaining combos easier. Combat fluidity: Adjustments to attack hit detection, cancel frames, and combo interactions have been made to improve hit detection, allow more combos to be continued, and make it easier to transition into other moves after a parry. Projectile speed: Projectile attacks will now decrease in speed upon hitting an opponent to create a more balanced competitive environment.
